Paste the embed into a Squarespace code block on the ticket product page, just above or below the Register Now button. It is fully self-contained (scoped styles, Raleway via Google Fonts) and deliberately has no button of its own — the native product CTA stays the only “Register Now”. When a rate steps up, update the product price field and delete the top row of the ladder.
